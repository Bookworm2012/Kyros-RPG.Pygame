A 2D RPG set in the world of Kyros. Fight monsters, find loot, get rich, and become famous . . . or die unknown

## How to Play
1. Make sure you have Python 3 installed (https://www.python.org/downloads/)
2. Download this repository
3. Open the Game folder
4. Open a terminal in that folder and run: python TextBasedRPG.py

## Features
- Explore the town of Elya
- Fight slimes in the field and collect loot
- Buy food and items from the tavern and shop
- Unlock the mysterious blacksmith
- Join the Adventurers Guild and take on quests
- Inventory system with equippable gear and consumables

## Tips
- Visit the field to earn Gold and find secrets
- Keep your health up by visiting the tavern
- Pay attention to any letters you find...
